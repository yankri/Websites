FAQ:

  • What's a "weatherghan"?

    A weatherghan is a crocheted or knit blanket where each row represents a day in a specified year. The color used for each row is dependent on the weather for each day for a given location. For example, if it is 89 degrees F or above, the row would be red.

  • Where does your weather data come from?

    Our weather data comes from the weather data records kept at major airports. The data is obtained through www.wunderground.com.
